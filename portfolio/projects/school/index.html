Elementary School Website

A complete educational website designed to improve communication, accessibility, and digital presence for a primary school.

Project Overview

This project involved designing and developing a full-featured school website that presents institutional information clearly while remaining easy to manage for non-technical staff.

Problem Statement

The school lacked a centralized digital platform for sharing announcements, academic information, and contact details, leading to communication gaps with parents and visitors.

Solution & Approach

A clean, responsive layout was designed with structured navigation, readable typography, and a content-driven approach prioritizing clarity.
